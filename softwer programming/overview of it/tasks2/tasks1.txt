
1. What is a Program? Give one real-life example.

A program is a set of instructions written in a programming language that tells a computer what to do.
It helps the computer perform specific tasks automatically. 
We use programs every day in mobile apps, websites, and computer software.

Example:When I use Instagram,the program allows me to log in, view posts, like photos, upload stories, and send messages.

4. Algorithm for Ordering Food on Swiggy

1. Start.
2. Open the Swiggy app.
3. Log in to your account.
4. Enter or confirm your delivery location.
5. Search for a restaurant.
6. Select the restaurant.
7. Choose the food items you want.
8. Add the items to the cart.
9. Review the cart and click **Proceed to Checkout**.
10. Select the delivery address.
11. Choose a payment method (UPI, Card, Net Banking, or Cash on Delivery).
12. Complete the payment.
13. Receive the order confirmation.
14. Track the order until it is delivered.
15. End.
