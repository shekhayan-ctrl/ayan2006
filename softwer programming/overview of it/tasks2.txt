# Software Architecture and Applications

## 1. What are the three main layers of software architecture? Write one line about each.

1. **Presentation Layer (UI Layer)** – Displays information to the user and accepts user input.
2. **Business Logic Layer (Application Layer)** – Processes data and performs the application's main functions.
3. **Data Layer (Database Layer)** – Stores, retrieves, and manages data in the database.

---

## 2. Define client and server in your own words with one real-life example.

**Client:** A client is a device or application that sends a request for data or services.

**Server:** A server is a computer or system that receives requests and provides the required data or services.

**Real-life Example:** When you open YouTube on your phone, your phone is the **client**, and YouTube's computers that send videos to your phone are the **servers**.
