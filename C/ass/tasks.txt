## 1. Name 5 IT job roles and write one line about what each role does.

1. **Software Developer** – Designs, builds, and maintains software applications.
2. **Web Developer** – Creates and manages websites and web applications.
3. **Mobile App Developer** – Develops applications for Android and iOS devices.
4. **Software Tester (QA Engineer)** – Tests software to find bugs and ensure quality.
5. **Database Administrator (DBA)** – Manages databases, backups, security, and performance.
